§ 1.0 Introduction

Introduction

I am a BS Electronics Engineering student (University of Santo Tomas, Class of 2027) with a systems-integration mindset. I am comfortable moving between the analog front end, the digital logic behind it, the firmware that drives it, and the network and infrastructure layer that makes it observable and maintainable.

Areas of applied interest include instrumentation and controls, embedded systems, analog and digital electronics, networking, and Linux/system administration. Coursework and independent projects (§4) have been used deliberately to exercise each of these areas rather than specialize prematurely.

Relevant coursework: Computer Networks 1 & 2, Microcomputer Systems and Design, Switching Circuits and Logic Design, Digital Communications, Communication Systems Theory, Signal Processing Theory and Applications, and Control Systems Theory.
